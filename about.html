About Rainydays & Contact



Rain clothes for all seasons

We Norwegians are proud to live close to the nature, that makes it important with quality clothing in all king of weather. From the winter season to the summer season we in Rainydays have all you need for an exciting trip.



Pushing the Comfort Zone

We are proud to give quality rain jackets to the people! Our slogan "Pushing the Comfort Zone" means that we will deliver the ultimate rain jackets or pants for your hiking trip as well as your simple camping trip in the mountains for a reasonable price. Rainydays was etablished with the idea that everyone shold be able to afford good rain jackets. The company was started by 3 students from NTNU in 2013 and is today growing each year. We are selling branded rain jackets for the Nordic climate and have also our own brand with more affordable prices.
